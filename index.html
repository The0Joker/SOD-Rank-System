League rules

Joining leagues

A clan admin assigns your league role based on your sign-up answers. Receiving the Ranked Style role puts you in RS. Receiving the True Power role puts you in TP. You can be in both simultaneously with fully separate stats and ranks.

Match validation

Both in RS → match logs as RS.
Both in TP → match logs as TP.
RS-only vs TP-only → blocked. Use /ascension instead.
If both players are in both leagues, pick the league with /pvp league:TP/RS.

Leaving a league

Use /leaveleague RS or /leaveleague TP. Your stats are preserved but you'll no longer appear on that league's leaderboard. The Elite title reassigns immediately if the Elite player leaves RS.

Elite RS title

Automatically held by the #1 S-rank RS player by points. Recalculated after every RS match — if you overtake the current Elite, you claim it instantly. Announced in the log channel when it changes hands.

Ascension — entering TP from RS

Win an Ascension match against a TP player to enter True Power. Command: /ascension challenger:X defender:Y score:X

Who can challenge

The challenger must be in RS only (not already in TP). The defender must be in TP. The defender's TP rank must be equal to or lower than the challenger's RS rank.

Win — entering TP

The challenger enters TP at whichever rank is lower between the two players' ranks. Their starting TP points = defender's current TP points + win points earned.

Loss — stay in RS

A loss means the challenger stays in RS only. They lose normal RS points as if it were a standard RS match. The defender's TP stats are unchanged — ascension results are logged but don't affect the defender.

After ascending

The challenger is now in both TP and RS simultaneously. Their RS stats remain intact. From here they play both leagues independently — future /pvp matches specify which league with league:TP or league:RS.

Point rules — same in both leagues

Points thresholds

F: 0 · E: 30 · D: 70 · C: 120
B: 180 · A: 250 · S: 330
(TP only) Legend: 420 · Angel: 520
GOD: challenge only

Points per win

vs 2+ ranks above: +25
vs 1 rank above: +18
vs same rank: +10
vs 1 rank below: +4
vs 2+ ranks below: +1

Points lost on loss

vs higher rank: −2
vs same rank: −5
vs lower rank: −12

Score bonus

3–0 sweep: +3 bonus pts
3–1 win: +1 bonus pt
3–2 win: no bonus

TP GOD tier rules

GOD challenge

Only one player can hold GOD at a time. Only Angel rank players may challenge. Beat the GOD → you become GOD, they drop to Angel. No GOD yet? First Angel to challenge and win claims it.

Same-rank matches

Multiple players can share a rank. To rank up you only need to beat one player at the next rank. Same-rank wins earn +10 pts.
